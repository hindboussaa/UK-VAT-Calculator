What is BMI?

BMI (Body Mass Index) is a measurement used to determine whether a person has a healthy body weight based on their height and weight. It is widely used in the UK by healthcare professionals.

A BMI value helps identify whether you are underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese. This tool gives instant results so you can understand your health status quickly.
