How the VAT Calculator Works

The UK VAT calculator helps you quickly calculate Value Added Tax on goods and services. The standard VAT rate in the United Kingdom is 20%, which is applied to most products.

To add VAT, multiply the original price by 1.2. To remove VAT from a gross price, divide it by 1.2 to get the net amount before tax.

UK VAT Formula

  • Net → Gross: Price × 1.2
  • Gross → Net: Price ÷ 1.2
